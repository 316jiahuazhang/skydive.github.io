THE QUESTIONS

Quick answers to customer questions

  • Is skydiving risky?

    yes , we have. But according to the National Security Council, the risk of this sport is lower than that of cycling.

  • Is there a weight limit for skydiving?

    yes , we have. The maximum weight is 115 kg. There is no minimum weight requirement, but in the case of children, the body must be able to wear parachute straps.

  • Is there an age limit?

    The minimum age for double parachuting is 12 years old, and for single parachuting is 18 years old. Skydiving participants under the age of 18 need to obtain the consent of their parents or guardians.

  • What are the health requirements of skydiving?

    There are no special health requirements. In addition to patients with heart disease, patients with severe hypertension, pregnant women.
